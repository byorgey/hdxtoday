and that this is not only detrimental to the child but to the adult's abili=
ty to understand and gain knowledge

<p class=3D"MsoNoSpacing">In the social sciences and in everyday speech we =

gs as agents. Just as folk psychology

r practice of attributing beliefs, intentions, and responsibility to groups=
 helps to shape groups and makes group action more predictable. =97 Dr. Deb=
orah Tollefsen<o:p></o:p></p>
