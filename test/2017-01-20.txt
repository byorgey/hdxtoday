<h2><span style="background:white">Sculptures by Mike Helbing<o:p></o:p></span></h2>
<p class="MsoNoSpacing"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNoSpacing">Hendrix is proud to present a solo show of artwork by Mike Helbing. Helbing is a Chicago based sculptor who has been an artist all his life. He studied Art and Art History at Ball State in Indiana and earned his MFA from Northern Illinois
 University. His experience as a soldier during the Vietnam War had a profound impact on him. He writes that after Vietnam &#8220;the world became surreal and art explained it.&#8221; Since 2002, Helbing has served on the Board of Directors of the National Vietnam Veterans
 Art Museum in Chicago.&nbsp; His has completed numerous commissions for public and private settings. His show will be presented Monday, Jan. 30 &#8212; Friday, Feb. 24 in Art A Lobby.<o:p></o:p></p>
