<h2>This Week's Project Pericles Forum: Russia and Election 2016<o:p></o:p>=
</h2>
<pre><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></pre>
<p class=3D"MsoNoSpacing">For individuals new to the Hendrix community, Pro=
ject Pericles is a program committed to promoting civic engagement among al=
l members of the Hendrix community and to enhancing civil discourse at Hend=
rix about the most challenging issues
 facing the campus and broader community. You will hear more about the prog=
rams of Project Pericles in the weeks and months ahead, but for more about =
Project Pericles at Hendrix, go to the
<a href=3D"http://www.hendrix.edu/pericles/">website</a>.<span style=3D"fon=

<pre style=3D"margin-left:13.5pt"> <o:p></o:p></pre>
<p class=3D"MsoNoSpacing">Now over a decade old, one of the ongoing program=
s of Project Pericles at Hendrix is the weekly Project Pericles Forum.&nbsp=
; Except when other events conflict, the Forum is held 12:10 p.m. &#8212; 1=
 p.m. on Thursdays in Campbell North. The topic
 of each week&#8217;s Forum&#8212;which may focus on a campus, local, natio=
nal, or international issue&#8212;is announced only a few days in advance s=
o as to maximize the currency of the topic. An informed facilitator frames =
the conversation and then quickly turns the spotlight
 to the members of the community to exchange their own views of the topic. =
The Forum is designed to encourage thoughtful and passionate, yet civil, di=
scussion.&nbsp; All members of the community are invited to bring your lunc=
h and join in the conversation.<o:p></o:p></p>
<pre style=3D"margin-left:13.5pt"> <o:p></o:p></pre>
<p class=3D"MsoNoSpacing">The first Forum of the year will be held today, S=
ept. 8, and will focus on Russia's engagement with the ongoing American ele=
ction. Russia has been accused of using electronic means to expose communic=
ations, hack into election systems,
 all for the purpose of, in the accusers&#8217; view, attempting to influen=
ce the upcoming elections in the U.S. Of course, Russia would not be the fi=
rst major power to try to influence an election in another country &#8211; =
during the Cold War, the CIA notoriously influenced
 elections throughout the world. This Pericles forum will explore some of t=
hese issues and the questions they raise: If true, could these Russian effo=
rts actually pose a threat to the democratic process in the US? Does it mat=
ter? And, in protesting these actions,
 is the US acting hypocritically?&nbsp;&nbsp; Dr. Daniel Whelan will facili=
tate the Forum titled: &quot;Russian Engagement in the 2016 Election: How W=
orried Should We Be?&quot;<o:p></o:p></p>

<h2>Zeuxis Group Show &#8212; &#8220;Flowers as Metaphor&#8221;<o:p></o:p><=
/h2>
<p class=3D"MsoNormal" style=3D"margin-left:13.5pt"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></p>
<p class=3D"MsoNoSpacing">Zeuxis is a grassroots organization formed in New=
 York City in 1995 to explore the contemporary possibilities of still life =
painting. To date, Zeuxis has organized over 50 exhibitions in museums, com=
mercial galleries, and other exhibition
 spaces. Reviews of Zeuxis exhibitions have appeared in The New York Times,=
 The New York Sun, The Philadelphia Inquirer, and numerous other publicatio=
ns.<span style=3D"font-family:&quot;Courier New&quot;"><o:p></o:p></span></=
p>
<p class=3D"MsoNormal"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></p>
<p class=3D"MsoNoSpacing">&#8220;Flowers&#8221; is a traveling group show c=
urated by Zeuxis. The artist and group member Gwen Strahle thinks of flower=
s as an &#8220;antidote against bad news and a cold winter.&#8221; Richard =
Castellana, another artist member, thinks of paintings as
 &#8220;visual metaphor.&#8221; This show demonstrates that the mundane and=
 often painted subject of flowers can be used to produce very diverse visua=
l interpretations that lead to a wide range of moods and styles.
<o:p></o:p></p>
<p class=3D"MsoNormal" style=3D"margin-left:13.5pt">The show will be Sept. =
8 &#8212; Oct. 7, at Art A Lobby.&nbsp; A reception will be held on Thursda=
y, Sept. 22, at 5 p.m.<o:p></o:p></p>
